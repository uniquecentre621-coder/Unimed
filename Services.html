Our Services

Comprehensive Primary Healthcare Services

From general consultations to chronic disease management and community health programmes — we are here for every stage of your healthcare journey.

What We Offer

All Services at Unique Medical Centre

Our practice is equipped to handle a broad range of primary healthcare needs. Below is a full overview of the services we provide.

GP Consultations

Whether you are experiencing a new illness, need follow-up care, or have a concern about your health, Dr Idugboe provides thorough general practitioner consultations. We take the time to listen, examine, diagnose, and develop a clear treatment plan for you. Walk-ins are welcome during consulting hours.

Walk-ins WelcomeMedical AidPrivate Patients

Medical Certificates & Sick Notes

Employers, insurance companies, and academic institutions may require a valid sick note or medical certificate. Our practice issues official documentation signed by Dr Idugboe following a clinical assessment. We ensure that all certificates are accurate, professionally prepared, and legally valid.

For EmployersFor SchoolsInsurance Purposes

Chronic Disease Management

Managing a long-term condition requires consistent monitoring, medication, and lifestyle guidance. We provide ongoing management for conditions including hypertension (high blood pressure), diabetes mellitus (Type 1 and Type 2), asthma, hypercholesterolaemia, and other chronic conditions. Regular follow-up visits help ensure your condition remains controlled and your quality of life is maintained.

HypertensionDiabetesAsthmaRepeat Prescriptions

Family Medicine

Unique Medical Centre is a family practice, meaning we care for patients of all ages — from newborns and children to adults and the elderly. Family medicine means understanding the whole person and their family context. We provide preventive care, immunisation advice, growth monitoring, and general health promotion for the entire family.

PaediatricsAdultsElderly CarePreventive Health

Referral Letters

When your condition requires specialist assessment or hospitalisation, we will prepare a comprehensive referral letter outlining your clinical history, current medications, examination findings, and the reason for referral. This ensures that the specialist you visit has all the information needed to continue your care seamlessly.

Specialist ReferralsHospital ReferralsClinical Summary

SASSA Assessments

We conduct medical assessments for patients applying for South African Social Security Agency (SASSA) disability grants. Dr Idugboe performs thorough clinical assessments and completes the required documentation to support your grant application. We understand how important these assessments are and treat all patients with sensitivity and care.

Disability GrantSASSA DocumentationClinical Assessment

HIV Counseling & Testing

Our practice provides confidential HIV counseling and testing (HCT) services in a safe, non-judgmental environment. Knowing your HIV status is the first step toward protecting your health and the health of those around you. If you test positive, we provide immediate counselling, referral for ART initiation, and ongoing support. All results are completely confidential.

ConfidentialRapid TestingPre & Post-Test CounselingART Linkage

Consultation Fees & Payment

We strive to keep our consultation fees affordable. We accept both medical aid and private payment options.

💳 Medical Aid Patients

We welcome patients from most major medical aid schemes. Please bring your medical aid card and a valid form of identification to your appointment.

💵 Private Patients

📋 SASSA & Government Patients

SASSA assessment fees follow standard medical guidelines. Please bring all relevant documentation including your ID and any prior medical records.
