What We Offer

Comprehensive Medical Services

We offer a wide range of primary healthcare services for patients of all ages, designed to keep your family healthy and supported.

GP Consultations

General consultations for acute and chronic conditions by a qualified and experienced general practitioner.

Medical Certificates & Sick Notes

Official certificates and sick notes issued promptly for employers, insurers, and schools.

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing management of hypertension, diabetes, asthma, and other chronic conditions with regular monitoring and prescriptions.

Family Medicine

Compassionate, whole-family healthcare – from infants to the elderly, everyone receives personalised attention.

Referral Letters

Specialist referral letters issued when further investigation or specialist care is required, with clinical notes included.

SASSA Assessments

Disability grant medical assessments for SASSA applications, conducted with care and thoroughness.

HIV Counseling & Testing

Confidential HIV testing and counseling services in a safe, non-judgmental environment, with linkage to care.
