Our Doctor

Meet Dr O.K Idugboe

Dr O.K Idugboe is a qualified and HPCSA-registered general practitioner with a passion for community medicine and accessible healthcare. As the founder and medical director of Unique Medical Centre, Dr Idugboe brings professional expertise and a deep commitment to the Mothibistad community in the Northern Cape.

At Unique Medical Centre, we believe that quality healthcare should be within reach of every person, regardless of their economic situation. That is why we have built our practice around the principle of providing quality and affordable medical care to all who walk through our doors — from chronic disease management to acute consultations, SASSA assessments, and HIV counseling.

Our approach is patient-centred: we listen, we assess thoroughly, and we provide treatment and guidance that addresses each patient's unique needs. Every patient is treated with respect, compassion, and professionalism.

Our Commitments

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To provide quality, affordable, and compassionate primary healthcare services to all members of the Mothibistad community and surrounding areas, treating every patient with dignity and respect.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and accessible healthcare provider in Mothibistad — a practice where no patient feels turned away due to cost or circumstance, and where every person receives the care they deserve.

Our Values

Integrity in every consultation. Compassion for every patient. Excellence in clinical practice. Transparency in communication. And a deep commitment to the health and wellbeing of the communities we serve.
